== My #### partition hasn't been detected by FSproxy but it's on "supported filesystems" list. ==
In that case it's a bug. [[MasterM Mail me]] the detection log (available at http://fsproxy) and full output of:
### file -s ## (for example: ##file -s /dev/sda1##).
If you don't know what that means or you don't have [[http://darwinsys.com/file file(1)]] on your system, **don't mail me**. Sorry, you won't be of any help.
